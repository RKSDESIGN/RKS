SELF-PROMOTION

BRIEF

RDESIGN is an interactive web design company based in Royal Road Port-Louis. Its roots lie in the founder's passion for drawing, culminating in a Graphics Design degree and the vision to provide international services. The company's core values—integrity, foresight, passion, openness, and responsibility—drive its commitment to delivering exceptional customer service through a positive company culture. The team comprises a web developer, accountant, animator, and assistant.

Their expertise enables RDESIGN to offer a range of services, including creating banner and logo images, designing websites, microsites, web pages, blog templates, themes, mobile apps, and various digital marketing assets like banner ads and social media content. The company's mission centers on delivering technically sound and affordable business solutions while prioritizing exceptional customer support. This mission guides them in designing print media, custom maps, multimedia, and websites.

Financially, they invest in internet facilities, computers, printers, and stationery to facilitate operations. Though they've already achieved educational milestones, their current client base primarily includes friends and relatives. However, their target market expands to new companies and SMEs, focusing on creating websites and additional services to cater to their needs. RDESIGN plans to build customer loyalty through marketing strategies like discounts, sales, and consistent follow-ups to ensure satisfaction and generate positive word-of-mouth referrals. Ultimately, RDESIGN's trajectory aligns with serving clients with top-tier services, expanding its client base, and maintaining customer loyalty through a blend of quality products, personalized services, and strategic marketing approaches.

3D SHOWCASE

The inception of the 3D showcase was aimed at presenting and exhibiting various stationery items in a visually compelling manner. Leveraging Autodesk Maya software, the stationeries underwent meticulous creation processes and were subsequently exported as FBX files. To enhance the three-dimensional appearance and immersive experience, these FBX files were imported into Twin Motion software. Through this platform, comprehensive displays were crafted, surrounding the showcased items within a dynamic environment.

Furthermore, the utilization of additional materials and textures significantly contributed to the lifelike and immersive quality of the 3D showcase. The development process involved dedicated efforts within the Twin Motion software, emphasizing the intricacies of design, composition, and visual storytelling to ensure an engaging and realistic portrayal of the stationeries in a three-dimensional space.


UNCONVENTIONAL ADVERTISING

The innovative advertising concept derives its essence from the compelling tagline, "We thread your web." This conceptualization draws inspiration from the inherent strength and intricacy of a spider's web, symbolizing resilience and precision in crafting tailored solutions.

The conceptual genesis revolves around the analogy of spiders meticulously spinning their own silk webs. Similar to these arachnids, the concept underscores the company's commitment to intricately weaving and customizing solutions, utilizing expertise and finesse to fortify and amplify their clients' online presence.

DENTAL CLINIC

BRIEF

Adilah Kader's journey into dentistry began with a childhood poetry competition focused on dentistry, sparking her fascination with the field. Her enthusiasm led her to pursue knowledge from local dentists, and her passion for the medical field blossomed during college. Eventually, she chose to study Bachelor of Dental Surgery abroad in Nepal. After five years of dedicated study, she established her own dental clinic in Saint-Paul Phoenix, Mauritius, inaugurated on May 14th, 2010. As a medical practitioner, she offers a range of dental treatments, including wisdom tooth extraction, sealants, and dentures.

Regarding the market position, the project aimed to address several key needs:

  • Promoting the Dental Clinic: To enhance visibility and reach within the community.
  • Creating a Promotional Package: Crafting offerings to attract and retain clients.
  • Raising Awareness for Dental Issues: Educating and informing the public about dental health concerns.

VILLA ROSHNEE

BRIEF

Absolutely! Villa Roshnee, a newly operational villa since November 2nd, 2018, finds its location in the renowned tourist-centric village of Grand Baie. Positioned strategically within close proximity to key landmarks, such as the SUPER U mall (450m) and La Croisette mall (1.4km), as well as the Grand Baie Bazar and the seaside (both within 800m), it offers convenience and proximity to attractions. Upon client request to promote the villa through diverse marketing strategies, the initial focus, as a Graphic Designer, involved the creation of an identifiable logo for Villa Roshnee.

Preceding the official launch, a teaser post was designed to generate awareness. Subsequently, a Facebook announcement was scheduled for the opening day, offering a chance for individuals to win discounted stays at the villa. A dedicated website will facilitate easy booking and room availability checks for prospective customers. Mall banners will be strategically placed to inform the local community about Villa Roshnee. Personalized newsletters will be dispatched to registered website users, keeping them updated on promotions and discounts offered by the villa.
